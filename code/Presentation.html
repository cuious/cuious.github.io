The programme combines study at University of Cambridge with field research in science and technology as well as project realization. Our services in 4 ways simultaneously: the advanced study of breaking frontiers of science and technology; creating a platform for international development; obtaining continuous technical services and high-end business introduction.

Throughout the duration of study we accommodate the individual needs of Chinese entrepreneurs and enterprises. Our services includes utilising technical services at the University of Cambridge, a research and development service, brand acquisition from overseas enterprises, participating in international conferences and trade fairs, setting up European office for Chinese companies and establishing research centre.

Follow-up service

Utilising the scientific and technological resources in Cambridge we offer a free service for our participants to recommend excellent research results and projects. We also help recommend scientific and technological talents in Cambridge to Chinese entrepreneurs who are interested in overseas investment in UK and Europe. Furthermore,professional network with alumni from our programme via our exclusive platform.
